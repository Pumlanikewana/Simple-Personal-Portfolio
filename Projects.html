One Piece Madlib Game

A Madlib game is a fun, word-based activity where one player prompts others to fill in blanks in a story without revealing the full context. The game begins with a template story with various words missing, which could be nouns, verbs, adjectives, or adverbs. Players are asked to provide these words based on their respective types. Once all the blanks are filled, the story is read aloud, often resulting in a humorous and nonsensical narrative.
